Hến xúc bánh đa

La river mussels are a specialty of Duc Tho Ha Tinh, which can be processed into many delicious dishes, the most prominent is stir-fried with sausages and eaten with rice paper. Do Trai beef noodle soup

Bun Bo Do Trai is famous for its large, round, slightly brown vermicelli like areca flowers, handmade by hand and a bowl of broth with a typical flavor of the countryside of Duc Tho, Ha Tinh. The vermicelli and broth are kept separate, the beef is soft, delicious, blanched in the broth, the blanched meat must be calculated according to the correct time, so that when eating, the sweetness is guaranteed. .
