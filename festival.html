  • Address: Cam Xuyen District, Ha Tinh Province
  • Time: The 8th day of the fourth lunar month every year

The Ngu Ong Festival originates from the belief of worshiping Ngu Ong with the desire to show gratitude and remember merit and pray to Ngu Ong to bless the wind and rain, and the sea to have plenty of fish and shrimp. Chan Tien Temple Festivities

  • Address: Chan Tien Pagoda, Tien Am Mountain, Thinh Commune Loc, H. Loc Ha, T. Ha Tinh
  • Time: The 3rd day of the third lunar month every year

Every year on the occasion of Chan Tien Pagoda festival, a large number of Buddhist monks and nuns, tourists from near and far come to the temple to attend, burn incense to pray for peace and luck. The festival also takes place many unique folk games such as fairy swing, tickling, wrestling, and cultural and artistic activities. In particular, visitors also have the opportunity to admire the Tien chessboard, the fairy's footprints engraved on the rock and enjoy the beautiful scenery from the top of Am Tien mountain with pine forests and majestic mountains.

Huong Tich PagodaTemple Festivities

  • Address: Huong Tich Pagoda, Commune Thien Loc, Can Loc District, Ha Tinh Town
  • Time: 4th to 9th of Tet every year

Huong Tich Pagoda Festival in Ha Tinh attracts thousands of people to participate in fairs, festivals, and Buddhist ceremonies to pray for a healthy and prosperous new year. In particular, participating in this festival in Ha Tinh, visitors can also immerse themselves in many interesting and interesting activities such as tug of war, wrestling, football, cultural and art exchanges and the conversion of ancient artifacts. excavated from Trang Vuong relic.

Dong Hoa fishing festival

  • Address: Dam Vuc, at the foot of Hong Linh mountain, Commune Xuan Vien, Nghi Xuan District, Ha Tinh Province
  • Time: Mid-week Sunday of the 5th lunar month

This is an extremely interesting festival in Ha Tinh with a special fishing experience that you should not miss during your trip. The traditional fishing festival is held in the spirit of agricultural and fishery extension to show solidarity and closeness to the people. Festival participants from all over will bring baskets, nets, rackets, ... and eagerly wade down to Dam Rac to catch fish after finishing the ceremony. According to the concept of Dong Hoa traditional fishing festival, whoever catches big fish will have a lot of luck, good harvest, expensive trade.

Ba Hai Temple FESTIVAL

  • Address: Ms. Nguyen Thi Bich Chau Temple (Ba Hai Temple), Ky La Wharf, Ky Anh District, Ha Tinh Province
  • Time: The 12th day of the second lunar month every year

Ba Hai Temple Festival attracts a large number of locals and tourists to participate in the festival, burn incense to ask for fortune, luck and peace. This is the death anniversary of Lady Nguyen Thi Bich Chau, held to commemorate and show respect to her. In addition to the sacrifices and incense offerings, this festival in Ha Tinh also takes place many exciting folk games such as boat racing, tug of war, ...

Hai Thuong Lan Ong Festival

  • Address: Hai Thuong Lan Ong Relic Complex, Son Trung Commune, Huong Son District, Ha Tinh Province
  • Time: 15/1 lunar year every year

This festival in Ha Tinh is held with the meaning of praying for health, national peace, and remembering the merits of the great physician Le Huu Trac. Coming to the festival, visitors will have the opportunity to visit the complex of Hai Thuong Lan Ong relic in Ha Tinh with poetic and lyrical beauty and participate in the ceremony of offering incense, memorial, releasing lanterns, ...
